Get to Know Me Better!

Hey there! I'm Yash, a tech enthusiast delving deep into the realms of Electronics and Communication engineering. From tinkering with circuits to diving into the world of digital design, I thrive on the excitement of merging technology with creativity.

My journey into the tech world began at DCRUST, where I cultivated a plethora of skills ranging from video editing and Arduino tinkering to PCB designing. These formative years instilled in me a passion for innovation and problem-solving.

My tech stack mirrors the dynamic landscape of modern technology. From Android development and Flutter to wielding the power of Java and After Effects, I'm always ready to take on new challenges. I craft sleek interfaces and seamless experiences using tools like Figma, ensuring that every project I undertake is not just functional but visually captivating.

With a relentless drive to stay at the forefront of innovation, I'm constantly exploring emerging technologies and refining my skills. Ready to embark on any project, I'm fueled by the thrill of creating solutions that are both cutting-edge and user-friendly
